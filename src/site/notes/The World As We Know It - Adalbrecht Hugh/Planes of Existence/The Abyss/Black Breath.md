Dense matter of [[The World As We Know It - Adalbrecht Hugh/Magic and Mana/Mana Types/Abyssal Mana\|Abyssal Mana]], often seen as black spots in [[The World As We Know It - Adalbrecht Hugh/Planes of Existence/The Abyss/Corruption\|corrupted]] individuals and as masses in high abyssal presence areas, emits this gas-like substance.
Consuming this substance in any form, including through breathing, can begin [[The World As We Know It - Adalbrecht Hugh/Planes of Existence/The Abyss/Corruption\|Corruption]] of a living being. However, in small amounts, the body's reserves of [[Mana\|mana]] can decompose it. As such, its consumption is considered a specialty drug in certain circles of underworld life, causing strong highs characterized by increased aggressiveness and reduction of cognitive function.
