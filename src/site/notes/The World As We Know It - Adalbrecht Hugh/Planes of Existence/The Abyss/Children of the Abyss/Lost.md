A type of [[The World As We Know It - Adalbrecht Hugh/Planes of Existence/The Abyss/Children of the Abyss/Children of the Abyss\|Children of the Abyss]] and the second stage of [[The World As We Know It - Adalbrecht Hugh/Planes of Existence/The Abyss/Corruption\|Corruption]], characterized by drastic reductions in cognitive capabilities and a high presence of [[The World As We Know It - Adalbrecht Hugh/Magic and Mana/Mana Types/Abyssal Mana\|Abyssal Mana]] density spots across the entire body, with notable presence in the brain.
These [[The World As We Know It - Adalbrecht Hugh/Planes of Existence/The Abyss/Children of the Abyss/Children of the Abyss\|Children of the Abyss]] will often be heard calling out for help or loved ones' names, mimicking the speech they found comforting in their lives.
