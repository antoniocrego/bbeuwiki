The Obsidian Veil guild is a criminal organization with presence in most nations, although principally and originally in [[The World As We Know It - Adalbrecht Hugh/The Nations/Veridian Reach/Veridian Reach\|Veridian Reach]].
The guild has been known to serve as a home for those who fail to integrate in society. Despite its criminal nature, it was deeply involved in the rebuilding of cities after the [[The World As We Know It - Adalbrecht Hugh/The General Timeline/PC 529 - The Francian War Machine kicks off\|invasions of Francia]]. To its members and some commoners, it is a necessary evil for balance in the world.
