An individual from [[The World As We Know It - Adalbrecht Hugh/The Nations/Azryt and Nyx Sombra/Nyx Sombra\|Nyx Sombra]], although the lands are uninhabitable, and as such no known people possess this nationality. This nationality does not refer to a formal nation, either, so it is mostly hypothetical.
Research expeditions to [[The World As We Know It - Adalbrecht Hugh/The Nations/Azryt and Nyx Sombra/Nyx Sombra\|Nyx Sombra]] have revealed that its inhabitants have become corrupted by the abyss, and as such, any [[The World As We Know It - Adalbrecht Hugh/Planes of Existence/The Abyss/Children of the Abyss/Children of the Abyss\|Children of the Abyss]] who roam these lands receive the prefix "Nyx-".
