**The Underdark** is an unofficial nation, instead being composed of multiple different focuses of power. These focuses are most often ruled by shady organizations, serving as a base of operations for them. 

**The Underdark** stretches far across the Lands of Gehenna, having originated from years and years of mining and exploration operations. As such, some locations are more directly controlled by the nation's government which resides above. However, both in these high control areas and low control areas, poverty is rampant, and so is discrimination.

It is said that [[The World As We Know It - Adalbrecht Hugh/The Nations/The Underdark/Underdarker\|Underdarker]]s do not know what the sun looks like, due to the underground nature of the pseudo-nation.

Dwarves are known to prosper in the never-ending mines of [[The World As We Know It - Adalbrecht Hugh/The Nations/The Underdark/The Underdark\|The Underdark]], forging their strength in the difficulties of daily life down below.
